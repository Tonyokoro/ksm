BRIEF HISTORY OF KNIGHTS OF ST. MULUMBA CALABAR METROPOLITAN COUNCIL

Knights of St. Mulumba Calabar was elevated to the status of Metropolitan Council on March 31, 1994 following the erection of Calabar Ecclesiastical Province and thus making Calabar a member of the Metropolitan Councils of the honored Order of the Knights of St. Mulumba Nigeria.

The young Metropolitan Council was headed by Sir Sylvester O’Neil KSM followed by Sir. William I.B. Okure KSM, Sir Mike E. Arinze KSM, Sir Dominic A. Ukpo KSM, Sir Diamond Ovueraye (GCKSM), Sir Mathew O. Achigbe KSM, Sir (Prof) Ndifreke P. Udonwa KSM and presently Sir Imo Ndaeyo KSM. Today Calabar Metropolitan Council is made up of 29 subordinate councils with Mamfe subordinate council in the Cameroun. Calabar Metropolitan council has its headquarters in Calabar precisely at St. Patrick’s College Ikot Ansa Calabar. Presently, Calabar Metropolitan Council is blessed with two Supreme Principal Officers in the persons of Sir Emmanuel Onyejekwe Deputy Supreme Knight and Sir Dr. George Eze Assistant Supreme Secretary. In the past Calabar Metropolitan council has been privileged to have at the Supreme level the following officers; Sir Henry Bello as Supreme Advocate, Sir (Prof) Anthony Emeribe as Supreme Secretary, Sir Andrew Okon as Supreme Advocate and Sir (Prof) Chris Nwamuo as Supreme PRO. Calabar Metropolitan Council under the leadership of Sir Imo Ndaeyo KSM is hosting the Supreme convention of the Order come Novermber/December 2024. To God be the Glory.
