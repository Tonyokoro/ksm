BRIEF ON THE 45TH KSM SUPREME COUNCIL CONVENTION

1. DATE OF THE CONVENTION: The SEB has approved that the KSM 45th Supreme Council Convention (Calabar 2024) should hold from Wednesday 27th November, to Sunday 1st December 2024, in Calabar, Cross River State.

2. THEME FOR THE 2024 SUPREME COUNCIL CONVENTION. DEEPENING CHRISTIAN SPIRITUALITY & FAMILY VALUES - ROLES AND CHALLENGES.
